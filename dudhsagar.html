TOURIST PLACES OF INDIA

DUDHSAGAR.GOA




Dudhsagar means "Sea of Milk", named for the milky appearance of the water.

Located on the Mandovi River in Goa, near the Karnataka border.

One of the tallest waterfalls in India, with a height of 310 meters (1017 feet).

The waterfall has a four-tiered structure, creating a majestic visual.

Best time to visit is during the monsoon season (June to September).

Popular for trekking, especially through Bhagwan Mahaveer Sanctuary.

The Dudhsagar railway bridge offers a dramatic view of the falls.

Trains on the Braganza Ghat section pass right in front of the falls.

Jeep safaris are available from Collem to reach the base in dry seasons.

Visitors can enjoy swimming in the natural pool at the bottom (non-monsoon).

Located within the Bhagwan Mahaveer Wildlife Sanctuary, rich in flora and fauna.

Featured in the Bollywood film "Chennai Express".

Road access is closed during the monsoon due to safety concerns.

Promoted as an eco-tourism site, supporting conservation efforts.

A favorite destination for nature lovers, photographers, and adventurers.
